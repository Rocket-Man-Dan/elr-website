About Us

This is Exotic Luxury Reviews. (Montage) This show is all about hunting down and filming some of the most expensive, exotic and luxurious things on the market today!

I'm Shawn and I live in Calgary Alberta, that's in Canada eh. During the day I hire, train and manage door to door and street sales teams. Sales and leadership and 2 of my biggest passions. My favourite part of my job is hiring some kid with no experience, and helping them grow into a self sufficient sales hobeybadger! Now, the skills I've learned from working in sales for the last 10 years have definitely helped me get to where I am today. But I'm just getting started, I have huge goals, and in order to become the man I need to be to accomplish those goals, I need to begin to surround myself with people who are already there.

Every day I'm going to be searching for Exotic and Luxury cars, waiting for the owner, and introducing myself and the channel. The goal? That we will be able to film a full episode with them and be able to pick their brains. Each episode will include a full review on the interior and exterior of the car, cold start videos, ASMR clips, epic driving shots at some of the most beautiful locations, and lots of conversations with the owner of the car.

Whether you already drive a supercar, or want to some day, this is the channel for you! So sit back, hit the subscribe button, and get ready. Cause this year, we're gonna fu@%ing send it.
