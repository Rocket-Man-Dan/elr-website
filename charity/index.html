Champstrong is a non-profit organization where we support, empower, and raise funds for amputees who need prosthetics. We conduct peer visits and provide education to amputees and their families, ensuring they understand that life is not over post-amputation.

We encourage good health practices and adaptive physical fitness as a form of both physical and emotional therapy. The Champstrong brand was created by an amputee for the amputees and supporters.

Our mission is to encourage, inspire, educate, and motivate everyone. We aim towards helping amputees realize that they have what it takes to have a new outlook on life and through daily challenges and hardships. Champstrong is more than a brand, it represents a lifestyle dedicated to raise awareness and inspire people from all walks of life. You cannot spell Champs without “amp”

About Me

Djibril Champ Kande is an incredibly inspiring individual who has overcome a great deal of challenges and adversity in his life. Despite losing a limb, he has not let this setback hold him back and has instead used it as a source of motivation to help others. He's a business owner, a volunteer at the hospital, coach for youth football, mentor in his community, model, currently working on a children book and founder of the Champstrong brand are all amazing examples of his determination and dedication to helping others. It's clear that he is a truly remarkable person and a positive force in his community and others.
