ELR Milestone Review

Mar 16, 2023

As we approach the end of our second month in operation, the ELR team wanted to take a few minutes to go over some of our milestones so far and to talk about what the future holds!

Exotic Luxury Reviews started as a “wouldn’t it be cool if…” idea in December of 2022. Before we knew it, that “What If” had become “Holy s**t we launch tomorrow!” Since then, it’s been a whirlwind of calls, planning, filming, editing, and brainstorming. (Not to mention liters upon liters of Tim’s coffee.)

So, what has happened since the January 15th launch? Well… a lot. Just ten days after launch we hit our first five-digit day, with over 16,000 people tuning in to ELR content. On March 7th we gained over 1,000 new followers in less than twelve hours, and yesterday we officially passed the 100k views mark on TikTok and YouTube! We're seeing exponential, organic  growth driven by our fellow Canucks, with scores of people from Canadian cities like Calgary, Edmonton Ottawa and Vancouver leading the charge. But what's really been the most jaw-dropping for us is that, from Indonesia to Israel, ELR has received viewers from almost every continent. (Anyone want to pass a link on to our friends in Antarctica?)

January and February have been huge for us, but this isn’t even the beginning. We’re officially launching the beta of the ELR website in March, with the full launch of “website 2.0” slated for May 1st. Website 2.0 will also feature the launch of our merch line, and we’re proud to say that all merch will be Canadian-made! In addition, we have several exciting partnerships with local Calgary businesses in the works that we hope to be able to announce soon.

Leading up to that we’ve also got some awesome content and giveaways in the works. Shawn’s first big review covering the 2022 Mercedes-Benz GLA 250 will be released soon, quickly followed by a review of a Ferrari Roma! And we’ll be giving away a PlayStation 5 once we pass 5,000 subscribers! 

Until then we just want to say thank you to everyone who has watched, followed, shared and subscribed. Without all of you this dream of ours would be, (quite literally), impossible. As I said, this isn’t even the beginning for ELR. Our 2023 calendar is already packed with exciting next steps for us. We haven’t scheduled much time for sleep, but there’s a Tim’s next door to ELR headquarters and they’ve got us covered.
